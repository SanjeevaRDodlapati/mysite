👋 Personal Introduction

I’m a passionate Software Engineer & AI/ML Researcher with over 6 years of experience at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biological sciences. My journey began with chemistry and evolved into developing cutting-edge AI systems that solve real-world problems in genomics, drug discovery, and healthcare.

Currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at Old Dominion University (GPA: 3.9/4.0) while simultaneously earning an MS in Computer Science from Georgia Tech, I believe in continuous learning and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with AI.
